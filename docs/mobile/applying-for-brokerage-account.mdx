## Creating a Daedalus Brokerage account

To get started, click on the `Invest` tab from the navigation menu at the bottom of the page.

### Full name

You must provide the full name as it appears on an official government identification

<Frame>
  <img
    class="h-96 rounded"
    src="/images/mobile/brokerage/name.png"
    alt="Full name"
  />
</Frame>

### Personal information

Next, you need to provide your data of birth, country of tax residence, and your primary sources of funding.

<Frame>
  <img
    class="h-96 rounded"
    src="/images/mobile/brokerage/personal-info.png"
    alt="Personal info"
  />
</Frame>

### Contact information

Please provide your `email` and `phone number` (with the country code)

<Frame>
  <img
    class="h-96 rounded"
    src="/images/mobile/brokerage/contact-info.png"
    alt="Contact info"
  />
</Frame>

### Address

Next, enter your current address

<Frame>
  <img
    class="h-96 rounded"
    src="/images/mobile/brokerage/address.png"
    alt="Address"
  />
</Frame>

### Citizenship information

Indicate whether you are a US citizen and if you have a social security number (SSN)

<Frame>
  <img
    class="h-96 rounded"
    src="/images/mobile/brokerage/citizenship.png"
    alt="Citizenship"
  />
</Frame>

<Note>

This information is secure and will never be shared

</Note>

### Statements and agreements

Lastly, you will need to confirm your following statements and agreements.

<Frame>
  <img
    class="h-96 rounded"
    src="/images/mobile/brokerage/statements.png"
    alt="Statements"
  />
</Frame>

<Frame>
  <img
    class="h-96 rounded"
    src="/images/mobile/brokerage/agreements.png"
    alt="Agreements"
  />

### Review

And you have officially submitted your application! The final step is just waiting for our system to review your application

<Frame>
  <img
    class="h-96 rounded"
    src="/images/mobile/brokerage/reviewing.png"
    alt="Reviewing"
  />
</Frame>

Once the review is successfully, you are officially registered with a Brokerage Account and can access the `Invest` tab
