## Alpaca

If you trade Crypto or Stock using Alpaca, you can connect Account to Daedalus to automate the trades.

Here's how you can connect your Alpaca account from the Daedalus app:

### Navigate to the Home tab

There, you can find a list of different platforms or exchanges to connect to.

<Frame>
  <img class="h-96 rounded" src="/images/mobile/home.png" alt="Home" />
</Frame>

### Click on the Alpaca listing

Here, you can find the option to `Connect to Alpaca`. Click on that button.

<Frame>
  <img
    class="h-96 rounded"
    src="/images/mobile/alpaca-screen.png"
    alt="Alpaca Screen"
  />
</Frame>

### Sign in using your Alpaca account

Log in to your Alpaca account using the Alpaca OAuth

<Frame>
  <img
    class="h-96 rounded"
    src="/images/mobile/alpaca-auth.png"
    alt="Alpaca Signin"
  />
</Frame>

### View your portfolio in Daedalus

<Frame>
  <div class="flex space-x-2">
    <img
      class="h-96 rounded"
      src="/images/mobile/connected-alpaca.png"
      alt="Alpaca Signin"
    />
    <img
      class="h-96 rounded"
      src="/images/mobile/portfolio-alpaca.png"
      alt="Alpaca Signin"
    />
  </div>
</Frame>

Congrats! Your Alpaca account has been connnected. Now, you can view your portfolio in the Daedalus mobile app and begin to automate trades.
